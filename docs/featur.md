# Agriapp - Product Requirements

## Core User Flows

### 1. Farmer Onboarding
- Profile setup and verification
- Land/field information registration

### 2. Land & Field Management
- Register multiple lands/fields
- Capture field details (size, soil type, etc.)

### 3. Crop Planning & Planting
- Assign crops to prepared fields
- Record seed/planting details

### 4. Field Monitoring (Ongoing)
- Regular crop health checks
- Growth stage tracking
- Issue identification

### 5. Water Management
- Irrigation scheduling
- Water usage tracking
- Reminders for timely watering

### 6. General Land Maintenance
- Maintenance tasks logging
- Record keeping over time
